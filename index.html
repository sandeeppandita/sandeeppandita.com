Few words about me

Working with computers has always excited me and I am still enjoying every minute of it. Being a web developer; I have worked on, delivered, managed and led projects of different scale on a variety of tech stacks. From static UI sites to eCommerce applications to full-fledged custom Content Management Systems; I have been privileged enough to be part of many amazing projects. When I'm not writing code, I'm improving my handcrafting skills, reading books, watching documentaries and stand up comedy shows, listening to audiobooks, hanging out with friends, and practicing yoga.

Being a developer is not only about writing good code but also about having the right mindset.
